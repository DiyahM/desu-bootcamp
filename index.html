MOBILE APP BOOTCAMP

July 5th - August 12th 2016

Jumpstart Your Career As A Mobile App Developer

Delaware State Mobile App Bootcamp is an immersive six week training program designed to teach you the fundamentals of building a mobile application.

Our training includes six weeks of intense study. In those six weeks, you'll learn the basics of building an Android app, in addition to learning Java, (the Android programming language). Classes will run from 9am-5pm, 5 days a week. But expect to spend as much time as you can outside of classroom hours working on projects and building your portfolio. Learning Java is not easy, and you’ll need the extra time. This is an immersive program. You will be provided with housing, meals, and laptops through out the duration of the program.

What You'll Learn

During the program you will learn the ins and outs of mobile app development from an Android point of view. You’ll learn software development best practices and industry standard tools. You’ll come out with a foundation in programming basics and a portfolio to build upon as you continue towards a career as a mobile app developer.
